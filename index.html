The Three Pillars of the Ecosystem

Nym: The Foundation

A quantum-resistant, anonymous block-lattice cryptocurrency providing the secure, private, and scalable ledger that powers the entire ecosystem.

QuID: The Identity

A universal, nomadic, and offline-first authentication protocol. Your single, sovereign identity for any system, secure against quantum threats.

NomadNet: The Application

A decentralized, anonymous social web platform. Own your digital space, enabling censorship-resistant content and a creator-first economy.

Ecosystem Architecture
