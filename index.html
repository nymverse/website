The Four Layers of the Ecosystem

Voyager: The Application

The flagship client to experience the decentralized web. Voyager is a beautiful, intuitive application built with the declarative Kryon UI language , serving as your portal to the network.

Axon: The Protocol

The decentralized social networking protocol. Axon defines the pathways for routing content and connecting identities, forming the nervous system of the social web.

QuID: The Identity

A universal, nomadic, and offline-first authentication protocol. Your single, sovereign identity for any system, secured against quantum threats.

Nym: The Foundation

A quantum-resistant, anonymous block-lattice cryptocurrency. Nym provides the secure, private, and scalable ledger that powers the entire ecosystem's economy and state.

Ecosystem Architecture
